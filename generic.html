Hotel Revenue Power BI Dashboard

Project Requirements:

1. Is our hotel revenue growing by year?

2. Should we increase our parking lot size?

3. Where are we seeing the highest growth with our hotels?

Completed report:

To find the hotel revenue data from the given data, I needed to create a measure which allows for easy aggregation within the dataset for visualizing the data.

Project requirements #1: Is our Hotel revenue growing by year?

The matrix table shares with us a continued year over year increase in both hotel segments. In 2020 we see a large dip in revenue this low demand was due to the pandemic which had a negative impact on travel.

Project requirements #2: Should we increase our parking lot size?

Based on the current parking percentages in our matrix table as well as the overall parking space usage at the hotels we see there is currently not a high demand for extra parking spaces. Creating new parking lots would not be the most efficient way to invest resources at the moment.

Project requirement #3: What trends can we see in the data?

The data above shows us the popularity of our Portguese hotels when we look at 2018, 2019 our Portguese hotel revenue has outpaced any other destination in Europe. Shifting to France throughout the years we can see our city hotels are the most popular destination for travelers. With Covid-19 restrictions being removed we could see a growing opportunity to invest in France to accomadate new guests.
